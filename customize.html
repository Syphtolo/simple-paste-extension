Simple Paste


You can customize your keyboard shortcuts and phrases below! For this to work, remember to press save! To make this work, set the phrase (what you want copied) and a keybind (what you press on the popup to get that phrase). The phrase limit is 10 characters (to prevent lag) and if you assign a keybind to multiple phrases, the last phrase with the keybind will be copied. If you don't want to use all 40 slots, you can just leave both settings blank!
